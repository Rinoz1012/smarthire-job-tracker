
🚀 Just launched SmartHire — AI-powered job tracker!
As a developer who has been through the job search process, I know how frustrating it is to apply for 100s of jobs and hear nothing back.
So I built SmartHire — an AI-powered job application tracker!
Here's what it does:
✅ Track all your job applications in one place
✅ Kanban board (Applied → Interview → Offer → Rejected)
✅ AI analyzer — paste any job description and get a fit score out of 100
✅ Shows exactly which skills you have and which you're missing
✅ Gives personalized recommendations to improve your profile
🎁 Special launch offer:
First 50 users get 5 FREE AI analyses!
👉 Try it here: smarthire-job-tracker.vercel.app
Built with React.js, Java Spring Boot, Groq AI & Firebase 💻
